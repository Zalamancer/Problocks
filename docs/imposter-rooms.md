# Imposter-Game Room Prompts

Image-generation prompts for the Among Us–style educational imposter game. Each room is a 1024×1024 top-down tile with pure chroma-key green (#00FF00) outside the walls for easy background removal onto the master map.

Shared conventions used in every prompt:

- **View:** strict top-down orthographic, 90° bird's-eye, no tilt/isometric/3-quarter.
- **Style:** chunky cartoon, thick black outlines, flat saturated colors, clean cel-shading, kid-friendly, no realism.
- **Passages, not doors:** walls have rectangular open gaps (~2 tiles wide) where hallways would connect. No doors, frames, arches, thresholds, sliding doors, or hinges — the wall just ends and the green chroma shows through.
- **Floor does not extend into passages** — chroma green starts immediately at the inner wall edge.
- **No characters, no readable text, no logos/brand names.**
- **Background:** everything outside the room walls AND inside every wall opening = solid pure chroma-key green (#00FF00), flat, no gradient/shadow/noise/texture.

Task-station convention: each room has a primary 2×2 tile spot (main mini-game) and a secondary 2×2 spot (alternate question type).

## 1. Cafeteria

Main social hub. Round center meeting table + emergency report button. Equivalent to Among Us's cafeteria.

```
Top-down orthographic 2D game tile of a school cafeteria, viewed straight
from above (90° bird's-eye angle). Chunky cartoon style, thick black outlines,
flat saturated colors, clean cel-shading, kid-friendly, no realism. Style
inspired by Among Us's cafeteria room tile but brighter and school-themed.

Layout: ONE large round central meeting table in the dead center of the room,
big enough for 8-10 characters to gather around it, surfaced in polished wood
with a ring of stools around it — this is the main gathering table where
emergency meetings happen. Around the edges of the room, place 3-4 smaller
rectangular lunch tables with attached benches, each a different cheerful
color (red, blue, yellow, green), clearly secondary to the central round one.

On top of the central round table, draw a bright red circular EMERGENCY REPORT
BUTTON — chunky, raised, with a glowing red dome and a white megaphone or
exclamation icon on top, clearly the focal point of the room.

Other room contents: serving counter along the top wall with trays stacked at
the end and sneeze guards, napkin and cutlery dispensers, two trash + recycling
bin pairs in a corner, a vending machine against one wall, a bulletin board
with colorful posters, a water fountain, checkered cream-and-teal tile floor,
soft butter-yellow walls with baseboard trim. Leave a clear 2x2 tile area near
the serving counter for a task station.

WALLS AND PASSAGES (very important, mimic Among Us exactly):
- The room is enclosed by solid walls with clean thick black outlines.
- Cut 2 or 3 rectangular OPEN PASSAGES in the outer walls where hallways would
  connect (one on the left wall, one on the bottom wall, optionally one on the
  right). Each passage is roughly 2 tiles wide.
- NO DOORS. NO DOOR FRAMES. NO ARCHWAYS. NO THRESHOLDS. NO SLIDING DOORS. NO
  HINGES. The wall simply ends and the green chroma background shows through
  the opening — just like an Among Us room tile where corridors are separate
  assets.
- Inside each passage opening, the floor does NOT extend outward — the green
  chroma starts immediately at the inner edge of the wall.

Lighting: soft overhead, no harsh shadows, everything clearly readable.
Perspective: strict top-down, no tilt, no isometric, no 3/4 view.
No characters, no people, no readable text, no logos.

CRITICAL: Everything outside the room walls AND inside every wall opening must
be SOLID PURE CHROMA-KEY GREEN (hex #00FF00, RGB 0,255,0). Completely flat, no
gradient, no shadow, no noise, no texture — one uniform green color for
background removal.

Square aspect ratio, 1024x1024, centered composition, sharp edges, high detail.
```
